Instructions
Tower Defense Game.  Use the towers and traps availible to stop
the creeps hitting your base
Click on one of the 8 images at the bottom to get that tower or
trap.
Towers are the first 4 and the other four are traps.
Click again on the map to place the trap or tower.  The cost
is written below the image
Be careful where you place the traps or towers as some spots
aren't allowed.
Click on the placed tower to upgrade or sell the tower.
The play button is used to skip the pause in between rounds
The pause button brings you to the menu, the 'M' key will
also do this
Your current score(which is also the money of the game),
round, map and health are in blue
We hope you enjoy the game and save the planet
